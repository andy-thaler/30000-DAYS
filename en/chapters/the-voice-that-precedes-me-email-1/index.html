The Voice that precedes me

Accounts from my mother about my origins.

(Note: Hulda Salum is the name of a prophetess mentioned in the Bible; this was the name she chose to use.)

From: Hulda Salum
Date: Aug 25, 2021, 5:00 PM
To: me

I entered into marriage on February 12, 1971. I had just turned 21 years old, the legal age of adulthood. It was done in secret; my parents did not agree with that union.

My husband was a third-degree relative by bilateral consanguinity, and the dispensation had taken one month to be granted by the Catholic Church.

We were married in a church called San Pedro Nolasco (which no longer exists today; there is now a glass factory there).

It was 8:00 p.m., and a cousin and his wife accompanied us as witnesses. From there, we each went back to our own homes; I said nothing to my fam at that time.

I worked in the oversight office for the airmail service and had to arrive early, since my supervisor was frequently absent and I was left in charge of the office.

My husband had just graduated as a police sub-lieutenant and was living in the Carabineros facilities because he was training there.

We continued this way for 15 days, carrying on with our lives without changing anything.

A work colleague offered me a room she had to spare, and we rented it. At that point, I had to speak at home to inform them of my marriage. My mother became very angry, and my father only cried. (Today, I see that scene as very sad. I disappointed my parents… but in my unconsciousness at that moment, it felt as if I were holding the sky in my hands.)

That same night, we moved into the room; we had no bed or anything at all. My husband obtained a cot and a mattress from the police.

This work colleague was a mature woman who lived with her husband, and on weekends I had to clean the apt. One day, a decorative item broke, and that became the reason we had to leave. I had never done housework or cooked before… but in life, you learn everything.

Contraje nupcias un 12 de febrero de 1.971. Acababa de cumplir 21 años, la edad para ser mayor de edad. Fue en secreto, mis padres no estaban de acuerdo con ese enlace.

Mi esposo era pariente en tercer grado de consaguinidad bilateral, y la dispensa habia demorado 1 mes en la iglesia católica.

Nos casamos en una iglesia llamada San Pedro Nolasco (hoy dia no existe, hay una fabrica de vidrios).

Eran las 8:p.m y nos acompaño un primo con su esposa quienes fueron los testigos. De allí salimos cada uno para su casa; y nada dije a mi flia en ese entonces.

Trabajaba en la interventoria ante el correo aéreo y debía llegar temprano, pues mi jefe se ausentaba permanentemente y yo quedaba encargada de la interventoria.

Mi esposo acababa de salir de subteniente de la policía y vivia en las instalaciones de Carabineros porque estaba formándose alli en Carabineros.

Asi continuamos 15 dias con nuestras vidas sin alterar nada.

Una compañera de trabajo me ofreció una habitación que tenia de sobra y la rentamos. En ese momento tuve que hablar en mi casa para informarles de mi matrimonio. Mi madre se enojó mucho y mi Padre solo lloraba. (hoy lo veo muy triste ese cuadro. Defraude a mis padres... Pero en mi inconciencia de ese momento, me parecia coger el cielo con las manos.

Esa misma noche tomamos la habitación; no teníamos cama ni nada. Mi esposo consiguio un catre y colchon en la policía.

Esta compañera de trabajo era una mujer madura, vivia con su esposo, y los fines de semana yo debia hacer el aseo del apto. Algun dia se rompió un adorno y esto fue la causa para salir de alli. Yo nunca habia hecho oficio, ni cocinado... pero de todo se aprende en la vida.
